# GREaT — Focus Group for Robotics Engineering & Technology @ FKEE UTHM

## 📌 About Us
A group from **FKEE‑UTHM** dedicated to advancing **robotics innovation, automation, and intelligent systems**.  
Our mission is to empower students and researchers to **design, build, and navigate** the future of robotics through hands‑on development, open‑source collaboration, and interdisciplinary engineering.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Advance research and development in **robotics engineering and automation technologies**  
- Provide **hands‑on learning opportunities** for students through reproducible lab flows and projects  
- Foster **open‑source collaboration** and knowledge sharing within the robotics community  
- Integrate **interdisciplinary approaches** combining hardware, firmware, and intelligent systems  
- Support **educational innovation** with accessible guides, visual roadmaps, and student‑friendly resources  
- Strengthen **academic‑industry partnerships** to bridge research with real‑world applications  

---

## 🛠️ Focus Areas
- 🤖 Autonomous mobile robots (ROS 2, micro‑ROS)  
- ⚙️ Embedded systems & sensor integration (ESP32, LiDAR, IMU, motor control)  
- 🧭 Simulation & navigation (Gazebo Harmonic, Nav2, SLAM)  
- 📚 Educational robotics (student‑friendly lab flows, visual guides, reproducible frameworks)  

---

## 🚀 Motto
**Innovate, Build, Navigate**

---

## 📂 Repository Goals
